Donating to Linux Mint

So far this month: $3,910 (166 donations)

We believe Linux Mint can gather the momentum and the community necessary to fund itself entirely through donations, sponsoring and advertisement without the need to engage in commercial activities and without focusing on anything else than improving the distribution itself.

The donations we receive range from a single dollar to a couple of hundreds. The important thing is for people to give something, and for us to generate the income that will let us work and succeed on this project, month after month.

If you want to help and if you can do so please make a donation to the project and encourage others to do so.
